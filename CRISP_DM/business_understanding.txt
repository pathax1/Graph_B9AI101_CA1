Objective:
Improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction by analyzing key metrics and uncovering insights across DART, LUAS, and BUS networks.

Key Business Questions:
1. Connectivity: How well are key urban and suburban areas connected?
2. Usage Patterns: What are the peak usage times for each transport mode?
3. Accessibility: Which areas have the most accessible stations and services?
4. Performance Analysis: How efficient are BUS routes and LUAS stations?
5. Integration Opportunities: Can schedules be optimized for better connectivity?

Use Case Example:
- Passenger Journey Mapping
- Operational Planning
